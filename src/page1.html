Stephan's Quintet: A Dance of Galaxies in Pegasus

Discovered in 1877, Stephan's Quintet is a captivating group of five galaxies in the Pegasus constellation. Four of these galaxies are about 290 million light-years away, while NGC 7320 is closer at 39 million light-years. The recent images captured by the James Webb Space Telescope reveal stunning details of their intricate interactions, showcasing the beauty of the cosmos.
