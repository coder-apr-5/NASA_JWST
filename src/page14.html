Neptune's Rings

On September, 2022, the JWST delivered the clearest view of Neptune’s rings in more than 30 years. Webb’s (NIRCam) captured several bright, narrow rings as well as the planet’s fainter dust bands.In fact, the methane gas so strongly absorbs red and infrared light that the planet is quite dark at these near-infrared wavelengths, except where high-altitude clouds are present.
