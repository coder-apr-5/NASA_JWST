Exoplanet WASP-96b

A transmission spectrum of WASP-96b was made by comparing starlight filtered through its atmosphere to unfiltered starlight. This is the most detailed infrared exoplanet spectrum to date. It reveals high accuracy and resolution of this hot gas exoplanet, which orbits a star 1,150 light-years away in the constellation Phoenix. The image also shows WASP-96b's satellite, an older star similar to our Sun.
